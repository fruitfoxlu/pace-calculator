How this pace calculator works

This pace calculator helps you quickly convert between pace (min/km or min/mile), time, and distance. It is useful for marathon pace planning, estimating finish time for a race, or checking speed for walking and cycling.

Common uses (long-tail searches)

  • Marathon pace calculator: estimate finish time for 42.195 km (26.2188 miles)
  • Half marathon pace calculator: convert pace to time for 21.0975 km
  • 10K / 5K pace calculator: convert time to pace (min/km or min/mile)
  • Pace to speed converter: switch between min/km and km/h (or min/mile and mph)

Examples

  • Given distance 42.195 km and pace 4:10 min/km, compute marathon finish time.
  • Given distance 10 km and time 00:40:00, compute pace in min/km.
  • Given time and speed (km/h or mph), compute distance for cycling or walking.

Notes: This tool does not account for terrain, elevation, heat, wind, fatigue, or fueling. It is a math conversion tool for pace/time/distance.
